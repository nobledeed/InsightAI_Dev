Insight AI Document Chat

Document chunks, embeddings, and vectorstores. Oh my!

This is an example of an AI assistant created to focus its abilities on a single PDF file.

Retrieval Augmented Generation-- it's a mouthful, but the notion is pretty straightforward: read in a document, a PDF in this instance, divide up the content into pieces (chunks), convert those pieces into something a language model can readily understand (vectors), focus a conversational AI agent on the vectorstore and viola! -- an AI assistant able to glean information from your knowledge base. RAG agents have a broad range of use cases. From effective customer service chat bots to domain specific research and analysis assistants, RAG agents are a cost effective way to bring the power of Generative AI to your organization.
