Insight AI Research Assistant

A Conversational ReAct Agent using Tavily and Wikipedia Search Tools

Have a conversation with a ReAct (Reason, Action) agent-- an agent designed to act as a research assistant. Created with a specific promt template, this agent will determine which, if any, search tool will best provide an answer to your question and then iterate over possible results before providing a final answer. It can respond to follow-up questions in an extended conversation.

Ask about the Gartner Hype Cycle, Learn about Portugal in the 17th Century, or discover unique things to do on a trip to Split, Croatia.

Your conversation history is stored in a SQlite database. You can pick up where you left off anytime.

The most fascinating aspect when coding this agent was puzzling out the prompt template. It blows my mind that writing functions to leverage AI requires a carefully crafted backstory and a defined role for an AI assistant:

Assistant is designed to be able to assist by providing in-depth explanations and discussions on a wide range of topics. As a language model, Assistant is able to generate human-like text based on the input it receives, allowing it to engage in natural-sounding conversations and provide responses that are coherent and relevant to the topic at hand. Assistant is constantly learning and improving, and its capabilities are constantly evolving. It is able to process and understand large amounts of text, and can use this knowledge to provide accurate and informative responses to a wide range of questions...
